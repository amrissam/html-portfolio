The Best Movies According to Amr

My top 3 movies of all-Time

Interstellar

I like Interstellar because it combines stunning visuals
with a powerful story about love and survival.

The Godfather

I like The Godfather because it masterfully combines family drama with
crime,showing loyalty, power, and betrayal in unforgettable ways.

Saving Private Ryan

I like Saving Private Ryan because it powerfully shows the courage and sacrifice of
soldiers making war feel real and deeply human.
